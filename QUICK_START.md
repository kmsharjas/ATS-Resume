## First Steps After Launch

1. **Home Screen**: Review the key features overview
2. **Create Resume**: Click "Create New Resume"
3. **Step 1 - Contact Info**: Enter your basic information
4. **Step 2 - Summary**: Add a professional summary
5. **Step 3 - Work Experience**: Add your job history
6. **Step 4 - Education**: List your educational background
7. **Step 5 - Skills**: Organize skills by category
8. **Step 6 - Review**: Preview and export your resume

## Common Tasks

### Add Work Experience
1. Go to Step 3
2. Click "Add Experience"
3. Fill in job details
4. Add responsibilities/achievements
5. Click "Save"

### Edit Skills
1. Go to Step 5
2. Click "Edit" on a skill category
3. Modify category name and skills
4. Click "Save"

### Export Resume
1. Go to Step 6
2. Choose export format:
   - **PDF**: Best for ATS, universal compatibility
   - **DOCX**: Editable in Word, good for ATS
3. Download and save

### Check ATS Score
1. Go to Step 6
2. View "ATS Compatibility Score"
3. Address any warnings
4. Re-check score

## Troubleshooting

### Resume Not Saving
- Check internet connection (for cloud sync)
- Ensure you have storage permissions
- Try refreshing the page

### Export Not Working
- Check that all required fields are filled
- Ensure phone number is in standard format
- Try a different export format

### ATS Score Too Low
- Review warnings in the feedback panel
- Add more skills (aim for 10+)
- Use stronger action verbs
- Add quantifiable metrics to achievements
